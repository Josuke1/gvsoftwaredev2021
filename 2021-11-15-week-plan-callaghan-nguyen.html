Including a blurb description for each assignment

Assignment:

  • The goal of this week's assignment is to write a brief description of each assignment that we have developed over the course of the semester
  • On the website, this will give the reader some context as to what the assignments are and the goals or motivations behind them
  • We will begin the assignment on Monday Nov. 22 and it will be due on Friday Nov. 26 at 11:59 PM (it should be completed well before this)
  • All submissions will be emailed to evan.callaghan@grandview.edu in the form of HTML scripts (one submission per group)
  • All submissions must follow the CSS style voted upon by the class (using the external CSS file provided by Craig on Nov. 8)
  • All submissions must match the format provided in our example post

Monday:

  • First, we will designate a previous class assignment to each student group.
  • The pair will then spend the class period discussing/writing a four to five sentence description of the assignment and writing a few sentences about how they interpreted/attacked the problem at hand.
  • At the end of the class period, every student group will share what they wrote for their designated assignment.

Monday Class:

  • First, we will designate a previous class assignment to each student group (see below for student groups and assignments)
  • The group will then enter a Zoom breakout room to spend the class period discussing/writing a description of the assignment and writing a few sentences about how they interpreted/attacked the problem at hand
  • At the end of the class period, every student group will share what they wrote for their designated assignment
  • Before the end of class, the group must email their assignment descriptions to their partner grouping (see partner groupings below)

Wednesday:

  • Students will spend the first five to ten minutes discussing their assignment descriptions and making the appropriate edits.
  • Then, the assignment descriptions from each group will be randomly distributed to another group for review, edits, and suggestions.
  • Again, we will randomly exchange assignment descriptions for review, edits, and suggestions.
  • After two other groups have made their edits, the descriptions will be returned to the original groups.

Next Steps:

  • After sending your written description to your partner grouping, you are responsible for reviewing, editing, suggesting the description that they send to you
  • Please add detail where the group may have left it out
  • The goal is to provide appropriate details as to what each assignment represents to the website reader, so it must be accurate and detailed
  • In total, the description should be between four and eight sentences, depending on the assignment
  • Make the edits and revisions, and email the final results back to the group (they are then responsible for submitting to the above email address)
  • Submissions are due before 11:59 PM on Friday Nov. 26, but it should not take this long to complete the assignment
  • Once we recieve a submission from each group and check to make sure it is detailed and is formatted correctly, we will create a pull request with all files
